== Description ==

My Online Fashion Store easily adds products from our app to woocommerce stores. The plugin provides banners for you to use for free, for your website or marketing needs.

No programming or technical knowledge required.

Easy access to Over 6000 Fashion Items immediately available for drop shipping from our warehouse in Los Angeles, CA to your woocommerce store.

You can add our app products to the store with increase/decrease price,also add combine products,multiple products add and remove products from woocommerce and easily check how many products sell from the my online fashion store plugin to the my order page.

Automatic inventory count update every 24 hours will keep the items you have added to your store updated & current. My online fashion store adds 100-200 new styles every week.

My online fashion store plugin is a paid plugin.you can easily buy a subscription monthly/year plan by a third party site for active plugin. change text to My Online Fashion Store plugin comes with a 14 DAY FREE TRIAL, with following plan options $29/month plan or $108/annual plan Our annual plan members also receive 20% discount on all merchandise for the entire year.

**At a glance, this plugin adds the following:**

* **Activation plugin:** For activation key, you have to purchase a monthly/year subscription plan form our [third-party site](https://subscription.ccdemostore.com/ ).Once successfully plan purchased,You will receive an email from us within 12-24 hours with your License Key to activate the plugin. 	
 
* **All Inventory:** Add our apps product to the woocommerce with product increase/decrease on the product Default Selling Price.Same products add as combined products on WooCommerce. Also, add multiple products and remove single/multiple products from the woocommerce store.Automatic inventory count update every 24 hours will keep the items you have added to your store updated & current.Pick & Choose which styles you want to sell in your store, or upload all available items with just a few simple steps.

* **My Inventory:** All added products listed on my inventory page. You can easily manage products from their page like single remove,multiple remove products.

* **My Orders:** Only shows those orders which my online fashion store products sell.

* **Marketing Material:** New banners uploaded weekly to the plugin for you to use for free, for your website or marketing needs.

* **My Account:**  You can checked current your activated subscription plan details.

* **Help/Contact Us:** You can refer Frequently Asked Questions.We are here to assist you & help you succeed. You can reach us by live chat, phone or email.

* **Free Returns:** Free return service provided to all our dropship members!

* **Quick Delivery Time:** Everything shipped from our warehouse in LOS ANGELES, CA.Orders shipped within 1-2 business days and delivered within 5-7 business days. Once shipped you will get a confirmation email with tracking info.(Time frame is for USA orders only)
			
== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* WordPress 5.2 or greater is recommended
* WooCommerce 5.2.0 or greater
* PHP 7.2 or greater is recommended
* MySQL 5.6 or greater is recommended

= Automatic installation =
1. Log In to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu, and click Add New.
2. In the search field type "My Online Fashion Store" then click Search Plugins or Press Enter.
3.Once you’ve found the plugin, you can view details about it such as the point release, rating, and description.you can install it by! Click "Install Now" and WordPress will take it from there.

= Manual Upload & installation =

1. Download the plugin.
2. Unzip it and upload to _/wp-content/plugins/_ 
3. Open WordPress admin - Plugins and click "Activate" next to the plugin

== Plugin Activation Steps ==

* Once the plugin is activated it will redirect to the activation page.
* On the Activation page, click on the purchase subscription plan it will redirect to the subscription site. On site purchase
monthly/year subscription plan and then You will receive an email from us within 12-24 hours with your License Key to activate the plugin. 
* Then fill the form and if the activation data are matches with our record then it will redirect you to all Inventory pages and you will be able to use plugin functionality.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Besides the WordPress APP fee are there any other additional fees? =
No, there are no other monthly fees we charge. There is NO per order fee or anything like that. Just the monthly WordPress APP fee.

= How quickly are the items shipped? =
All orders are shipped within 1-2 business days from the day you place your order, and delivered within 5-7 business days from the day you place the order. Once the order is shipped you will get a confirmation email with tracking info.
Shipping info: https://www.myonlinefashionstore.com/pages/shipping-info
Time frame is for USA orders only.

= Do you ship outside the US? =
Currently we can drop ships within the USA only. What our international partners do is they find a shipping partner here in the USA, we will ship the packages to them, the shipping company will either forward the package to you or forward the package to your customer.

= Can I cancel anytime & when do I get billed? =
Yes, there are no long term contracts, you can cancel anytime, you will be charged after your 14 day FREE trial.

= Where is your company located? =
We are located in Los Angeles, CA - all orders will be shipped from our warehouse in Los Angeles, CA

= Do you have stock of the items available for sale? =
Yes, all the items you see available in our APP you will have access to. The items are in stock, ready to ship from our warehouse in LOS ANGELES, CA.

= Do we have access to all the products? =
Yes, you will have access to all the products in our inventory - currently we have over 4000 items available. New items are added every week!

= Can we pick which items we want to sell? =
Yes, you can pick and choose which items you want to sell in your store.

= Is there a minimum order? =
No, there is no minimum dollar amount or quantity amount you have to order. As a dropship member your account will be set up so you can order from our wholesale website ccwholesaleclothing.com without the minimum $100 per order requirement.

= How do we check inventory count? =
You will be able to see the total quantity available under the thumbnail image of each product in the APP. Once an item is uploaded to your store, inventory SYNC will run daily and update the stock count automatically.

= How do we place orders? =
Currently you do have to place the order manually on our wholesale website ccwholesaleclothing.com. If you are going to process more than 10 orders a day, we can offer a complimentary service where you can supply a spreadsheet file of your orders daily by email and we will take care of the rest for you.

If you are processing less than 10 orders a day please follow the steps below.

Please make sure you create an account on our wholesale website ccwholesale clothing.com notify us once an account has been created so we can change your account status to dropship account.

Once you receive an order, go to ccwholesale clothing.com and place the order to be blind shipped to your customer.

Min $100 requirement per order will be removed once your account is set up on ccwholesaleclothing.com.
 Login to your account 
 Select the items to order 
 Go thru the checkout process 
 Put your information in the billing area & your customers Name/Address in the shipping area 
 Pay for the order &amp; You Are Done! We will take care of the rest. 

When searching for an item on ccwholesaleclothing.com to purchase for your customer please see instructions below
 
example. you have an order for sku: 34233b.1xl 
instead of searching for 34233b.1xl 
only type in the search box 34233b or 34233 please leave out .1xl

you can get more info regarding shipping info on this page below 
https://www.myonlinefashionstore.com/pages/shipping-info 

= How often do you add new items? =
New items are added every week, we add close to 100 - 200 new styles weekly.

= How much can I sell items for? =
You have total control over how much you want to resell the items for. By default the retail price is set at 2X your cost for the item. This price is displayed in the APP as “DEFAULT SELLING PRICE” We recommend staying around this retail price to maximize your sales and conversions.

= What is going to be my cost for the items? =
Your cost for the items are displayed in the APP ( under the thumbnail of the product you will see “YOUR COST”) You can also visit our wholesale website ccwholesaleclothing.com to view all our items currently in stock and your cost.

= Do you restock items? =
Yes we restock items minimum twice a week. You can view restocked items under the RESTOCKED POPULAR ITEMS category. Because we restock items twice a week, we do recommend NOT to delete the items from your store, instead setup your collections to HIDE items when quantity reaches “0”, it is simply adding a conditional to your collections - if you are not sure how to set this up please contact WIX support for assistance. This way when the item is restocked it will automatically update inventory in your store and be available for your customers again.

= Can we combine multiple colors of the same item into one product? =
You can combine items from the same style.
For example if an item has 5 colors you can select all 5 items scroll up and click on “COMBINE PRODUCTS &amp; ADD” – this way the item will add to your store as a single SKU with option for your customer to choose both size &amp; color.

= Do you provide marketing material? =
 Yes, within the app you can go to the section MARKETING MATERIAL and download banners from there. We list banners with both text and without text. You can add 
 your own text to the banners by using simple FREE paint programs that come with all computers or any online image editing program. 

= Can you include my company info in the shipments? =
 Yes, if you have marketing material like stickers/flyers/postcards you can ship those to us and we can include it along with your orders. 
 For more info regarding packing &amp; shipping please see the link below. 
Packing info: https://www.myonlinefashionstore.com/pages/packing-info 
Shipping info: https://www.myonlinefashionstore.com/pages/shipping-info 

= How much is shipping? =
 The shipping cost is based on the quantity of items in order, below is our shipping rates. 
 1 item: $3.75 
 2-3 items: $4.75 
 4-5 items: $5.75 
 6-7 items: $6.75 
 8-9 items: $7.75 
 10 or more items: $8.75 
 All orders shipped priority mail - delivery time is 5-7 business days. 
 All orders shipped from LOS ANGELES, CA 

= Do you offer return service? =
  Yes, we offer free UPS return labels for our dropship partners. 
 Please click on the link below for more info regarding our return policy. 
https://www.myonlinefashionstore.com/pages/free-returns 
 Please click on the “RETURNS” tab in this APP to fill out the return form. 

= Are your items true to size? =
Yes, our items are true to size. We source our items from local manufactures based out of mostly in LOS ANGELES, CA – so the items we carry are manufactured for the USA MARKET and are true to size &amp; good quality.

= What brands do you carry? =
We carry many different brands mostly sourced from local manufacturer companies based out of Los Angeles, CA

= Is your company info on the packages, product or shipping label? =
No, our company info is not on any product, package or shipping label. Please click on the link below for more shipping info and an example of the shipping label.
https://www.myonlinefashionstore.com/pages/shipping-info

= Your wholesale website says minimum $100, do I have to order $100 every order? =
No $100 minimum is for our regular wholesale customers, once you have a dropship account with us, the $100 minimum requirement is removed from your account.

= How does packaging look when shipped to the customers? =
Not only will we make sure your customer gets their order in a timely manner but also in a presentable and excellent condition! See below for more info on packing. https://www.myonlinefashionstore.com/pages/packing-info

= Can I add more than one item at a time to my store? =
Yes you can select up to 90 items at a time and add them at once.
Just change the “30 per page” option to “90 per page” select the items you want to add, scroll up and click on ADD TO WIX.
